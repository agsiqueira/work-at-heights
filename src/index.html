Step 1

Orientation

Enter your information to personalize the learning track. Then open SmartChats, watch the module videos, ask questions, and download the NR-35 ebook for review.

Set up your learning track

Your name will appear on reports and the certificate. Your email will be used to personalize the SmartChats link and match your exported interaction logs later.

The SmartChats button becomes active after a valid name and email are entered.

Before continuing

  1. Watch the required SmartChats module videos.
  2. Ask questions about concepts that are unclear.
  3. Export your SmartChats interaction analytics as a CSV file.
  4. Return here to continue into the adaptive interview and quiz.

Step 3

Interview

Conversational Safety Readiness Interview

Choose your Senior Safety Instructor

This is not a traditional quiz. During this conversation, the selected Instructor will ask you to explain safety-related decisions in your own words, adapt the difficulty based on your responses, and recommend relevant ebook chapters when additional review would be beneficial.

Select a portrait to choose the instructor voice, personality, and conversational style for the interview.
